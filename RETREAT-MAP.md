## Vetting framework — the desk's checklist
1. **Screening rigor** — medical/psych intake; contraindications enforced (psychedelics:
   SSRIs, lithium, psychosis history; fasting: refeeding risk, cardiac; intensive
   meditation: psychiatric history).
2. **Medical & emergency capability** — on-site medical, hospital distance, protocols.
3. **Facilitator verifiability** — training/lineage checkable, named references,
   misconduct search clean, facilitator-to-guest ratio, power-dynamics awareness.
4. **Integration support** — preparation calls, post-program follow-up. The single best
   marker separating transformational from destabilizing.
5. **Legal & insurance posture** — jurisdiction legality, liability, incident handling.
6. **Marketing vs. evidence** — outcome claims proportionate to data.
7. **Privacy practice** — group composition, NDAs available, media policy (HNW critical).

## Risk awareness — what the radar watches
- Facilitator misconduct allegations (recurring across lineages; existential risk for a
  placement desk).
- Deaths/serious incidents: ayahuasca cardiac interactions, sweat-lodge heatstroke,
  unsupervised fasting.
- Meditation-related adverse effects: Willoughby Britton (Brown), Cheetah House casework —
  intensive retreats destabilize a meaningful minority; screen accordingly.
- Jurisdiction shifts: crackdowns and legalizations move the psychedelic retreat map.
